The St. Regis New York

Located on Fifth Avenue, the St. Regis New York is a timeless symbol of luxury, offering elegant rooms with classic decor, marble bathrooms, and 24-hour butler service. Built in 1904, it blends historic charm with modern sophistication and is home to the legendary King Cole Bar, where the Bloody Mary was born.

W New York Times Square

Set in the heart of Times Square, the W New York offers a bold, modern stay with sleek design, floor-to-ceiling views, and a high-energy atmosphere. It's a favorite for those seeking a vibrant urban experience with on-site nightlife, stylish lounges, and cutting-edge amenities.

The Plaza Hotel

A New York icon since 1907, The Plaza combines Gilded Age grandeur with contemporary luxury. Overlooking Central Park, the hotel features opulent suites, world-class dining, the famed Palm Court for afternoon tea, and a location steeped in history and elegance.

Four Seasons Hotel New York

Designed by I.M. Pei, this sleek Midtown tower offers understated luxury and sweeping views of the city. Known for its spacious suites, impeccable service, and serene atmosphere, the Four Seasons is a refined retreat just steps from Central Park and top attractions.
