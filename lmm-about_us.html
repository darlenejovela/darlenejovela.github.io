LAST MINUTE MEMORIES


Who are we?


Our Vision

Our vision is to be the first choice for travelers who make decisions in the greatest city in the world—on a whim, with purpose. We believe last-minute adventures should feel just as smooth and exciting as well-planned ones. Just like the energy of New York, we’re non-stop in our pursuit of offering value, spontaneity, and unforgettable experiences. We exist to help people say yes to the moment—and let the city blow us all away.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve spontaneous travelers who are not throwing away their shot at a last-minute adventure. We provide quick, hassle-free bookings, exclusive deals, and curated recommendations for New York City’s top attractions—because when you're young, scrappy, and hungry, your travel experience should still be smooth and high-quality. With strong partnerships, local insights, and a passion for service, we make sure even last-minute plans are well-executed, helping you rise up and make the most of every opportunity.

Our Goal
  • To provide unbeatable last-minute travel deals to New York City—because sometimes the best plans are made in the eye of a hurricane.
  • To recommend iconic sights and local gems, so you don't just visit the city—you experience it, room where it happens and all.
  • To help travelers act quickly and confidently, so they're never waiting for it when the opportunity to explore arises.
